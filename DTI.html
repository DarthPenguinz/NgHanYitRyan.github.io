Transformable Stools / Park Bench and Table

Our final product were cuboid stools which could be transformed into benches and tables of varying heights. They were also labelled with crosses and circles as they can be used as tic-tac-toe elements. I led my team with the ideation process as well as the construction of the prototypes.
