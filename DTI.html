February - May, 2022

Design Thinking & Innovation

Our final product were cuboid stools which could be transformed into benches and tables of varying heights. They were also labelled with crosses and circles as they can be used as tic-tac-toe elements. I led my team with the ideation process as well as the construction of the prototypes.

The first design we had was to make simple stools to be used as tic-tac-toe pieces.

The second idea designed was for transforming stools to form benches and tables.

We eventually decided on having 2 seperate types of cubes, the first is unfoldable.

It can be seperated into a smaller plank and a large one.

We can then form a short bench/table using 2 fixed shaped cubes along with a small plank.

We can also form a long bench/table using 2 fixed shaped cubes along with a large plank.

Using just 8 stools, we were able to create a large table for use.

In the site, we plan to have much more stools so that there are more than enough available.
