Money Tracking Application
Using Google Appsheet

While looking for an application to help me track my spending, I was unable to find any application I liked. Many either had too little functions, or too many that it made it un-intuitive to use. Hence, with the use of Google Appsheet, I was able to customise my own application to meet my personal needs. The home page starts with an overview of my monthly spending and Total Yearly spending.

By clicking on my profile, I will be able to view the monthly breakdown for the year.

On the Transactions tab, I will be able to view a full list of individual expenditures (No categories).

On the List Tab, I can view my expenditures and their type.

From List Tab, I can order them based on Date or Time.

By clicking on a specific expenditure, I will be able to see the full expenditure details.

To add new transaction, simply fill in the details. Those with a * cannot be blank.
